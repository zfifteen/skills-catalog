Thought for 3s
Prime Gap Structure (PGS) studies the integers between consecutive primes p and q as an ordered divisor-count field d(n). The prime gap interior is the sequence of composite d(n) values (all >2) between p and q.⁠GitHub
The Gap Winner Rule (GWR) states: the leftmost composite carrying the minimal d(n) in the gap is the raw-Z maximizer (via DNI: Z(n) = exp(-(d(n)/2 - 1) ln n), primes at Z=1) and the structurally selected interior witness.⁠GitHub
Proved result: No-Later-Simpler-Composite Theorem — once the minimal d(n) appears, no simpler (lower d) composite occurs later in the gap (zero violations through 10¹⁸).⁠GitHub
Repo: https://github.com/zfifteen/prime-gap-structure (see PROOF.md).
